Pie is a convention about sharing pieces of information between devices. It doesn’t depends on device’s brand or OS, because we believe that talking is fluid.

Talking is fluid and fun

Humans communicate naturally. Nobody cares about the “OS version” of a friend’s ear. One speak, another one understands — things are simple as that. But to our devices, talking is far complicated.
