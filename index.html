OpenPie: using JSON + Schema.org vocabulary to
bring freedom to devices and developers

When people talk, things are quite simple and fun. But the same is not true to humans’ devices. The "pie" approach switchs the all-is-about-OS mindset to a new one, adding a layer of freedom and fun to devices.
